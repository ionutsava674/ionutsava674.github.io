We do not collect personal identification information such as your name, email address, or contact details unless you voluntarily provide it to us when contacting support or providing feedback.

1.2 Usage Data

When you access the App, we may collect certain information automatically, including but not limited to:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, operating system)
  • Usage statistics (e.g., features used, session duration)
  • Crash reports and performance data

1.3 Data Stored via CloudKit

The App uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your game progress across your devices. Data stored includes:

  • Game progress and settings

This data is associated with your iCloud account but is not accessible by us. It is stored securely on Apple's servers, and we do not have access to your personal iCloud information.
