Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the purpose of this app?

The app is designed to help rock climbers, especially those with visual impairments, improve their memory and spatial awareness by memorizing climbing routes. It provides a virtual environment where users can practice recalling sequences of moves, enhancing their cognitive skills for real-world climbing.

2. How do I start using the app?

To begin, select the number of moves you want to memorize and choose your desired difficulty level. The app will generate a virtual climbing route for you to study. After memorizing the route, you'll be prompted to input each move in the correct order.

3. What are the different difficulty levels?

The difficulty levels are:

  • Direction Only: Memorize the direction of each move.
  • Direction and Distance: Memorize both the direction and the distance (near, normal, far) of each move.
  • Direction, Distance, and Hand Side: Memorize the direction, distance, and whether to use your left or right hand.
  • Direction, Distance, and Hand or Foot: Memorize the direction, distance, and whether to use your hand or foot.

4. How does the progression system work?

The app features a tiered progression system with increasing difficulty. You start with simple routes and advance by completing levels with consecutive correct attempts. As you progress, the sequences become longer, and more movement details are added to enhance the challenge.

5. Can I customize the number of moves in a route?

Yes, you can select the number of moves you'd like to practice with. This allows you to tailor the difficulty and length of each session to your personal preference and training needs.

6. Is the app suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! The app is designed for climbers of all skill levels. Beginners can start with shorter sequences and simpler difficulty settings, while more advanced climbers can challenge themselves with longer routes and additional movement details.

7. Does the app support visually impaired users?

Yes, the app is optimized for users with visual impairments. It includes accessibility features such as VoiceOver support and high-contrast visuals to ensure that all climbers can benefit from the training exercises.

8. How can I track my progress?

The app provides real-time feedback after each session, showing your success rate and areas for improvement. It also keeps a record of your achievements, so you can monitor your progress over time.

9. Can I sync my game progress across multiple devices?

Yes, the app integrates with Swift Data CloudKit to automatically save your game progress to the cloud. This means you can continue any game on any of your devices seamlessly.

10. Do I need an internet connection to use the app?

While you can use the core features of the app offline, an internet connection is required to sync your game progress across devices using CloudKit. Without an internet connection, your progress will be saved locally and synced once you're back online.

11. Is my data safe and private?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All your data is securely stored using Apple's CloudKit services, and we do not collect personal information without your consent. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more details.

12. Are there any social features?

Currently, the app focuses on individual training. However, we are working on adding social features in future updates, such as the ability to share your progress and challenge friends.

13. How do I report a bug or provide feedback?

We appreciate your feedback! You can report bugs or send suggestions by emailing us at support@yourapp.com. Your input helps us improve the app.

14. Is there a user guide or tutorial?

Yes, the app includes a user guide accessible from the main menu. It provides detailed instructions on how to use all the features and get the most out of your training sessions.

15. How can I support the development of the app?

You can support us by sharing the app with others who might find it useful and by leaving a positive review in the app store. Your encouragement helps us continue improving the app.
