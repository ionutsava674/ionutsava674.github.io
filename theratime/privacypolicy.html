Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 6, 2024

At TheraTime, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our services.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

TheraTime is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or sensitive information. The app operates entirely on your device, and all settings and data remain locally stored.

2. Use of Health and Fitness Data

TheraTime utilizes WatchOS background modes intended for health and fitness activities to provide continuous haptic feedback during your sessions. This functionality does not require access to your personal health data, and no such data is collected or processed by the app.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your information. Since TheraTime does not collect or transmit any data, there are no security risks associated with data breaches or unauthorized access.

5. Children's Privacy

TheraTime is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
