How It Works

TheraTime utilizes gentle haptic feedback to help you manage your massage sessions effortlessly. Here's how to get started:

  1. Set Up Your Session: Open the TheraTime app on your Apple Watch and customize your session length and sequences according to your workflow.
  2. Start the Session: Tap 'Start' to begin. The app runs in the background, allowing you to focus entirely on your client.
  3. Receive Haptic Cues: Feel subtle vibrations indicating when to switch areas or when the session is nearing completion.
  4. End the Session: The app alerts you when the session time is up. You can then end or extend the session as needed.
